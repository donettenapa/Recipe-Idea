	 <p><h1><font face="Castellar" size="10" color="7CFC00">A Vegetarian Recipe</font></h1>
	 <p><font face="Segoe Script" size="7" color="AFE1AF">Baked Apples and Ice Cream</font></p>
	 <hr width="800" size="5" color="7CFC00"/>
	 
	 
	 <p><font face="georgian" size="5" color="white">Prep time: 20 minutes</font></p>
	 
	 <p><font face="Showcard Gothic" size="6" color="white">INGREDIENTS:</font><br>	 
	 <ul><font face="georgian" size="5" color="white">
		 <li>1 apple, cored</li>
		 <li>2 tbsp raisins</li>
		 <li>1/2 cup light vanilla ice cereal</li>
		 <li>pinch of cinnamon</li></font>
	 </ul>
	 
	 <p><font face="Showcard Gothic" size="6" color="white">UTENSILS:</font><br>
	 <ul><font face="georgian" size="5" color="white">
		 <li type="square">apple corer (you'll need help from your adult assistant)</li>
		 <li type="square">measuring spoons</li>
		 <li type="square">measuring cup</li>
		 <li type="square">oven (you'll need help from your adult assistant)</li></font>
	 </ul>
	 
	 <hr width="800" size="5" color="7CFC00"/>
	 
	 <p><font face="Showcard Gothic" size="6" color="white">DIRECTIONS:</font><br>
	 <ol><font face="georgian" size="5" color="white">
		 <li>Take a cored apple and cut it in half.</li>
		 <li>Sprinkle raisins over the apple</li>
		 <li>Place on a baking sheet and bake it in the oven for 15 minutes at 350°F(176°C)</li>
		 <li>Remove the sheet from the oven and put a pinch off cinnamon over each apple half.</li>
		 <li>Place the ice cream over the apple halves.</li></font>
	 </ol>
